Samsung Galaxy Watch8 Classic (46mm Bluetooth, Black)

The Samsung Galaxy Watch8 Classic combines premium design with advanced health and fitness technology. Built with a durable stainless steel body and sapphire glass display, this smartwatch offers a classic watch appearance with powerful modern features. The 3nm processor ensures faster performance, smooth navigation, and efficient battery usage for everyday tasks and workouts.

Equipped with advanced health monitoring tools such as Blood Pressure, ECG, Irregular Heart Rhythm Notification (IHRN), and Vascular Load monitoring, the watch helps track your health in real time. Dual GPS provides accurate location tracking for outdoor activities like running, cycling, and hiking. With 5ATM and IP68 water resistance, the Galaxy Watch8 Classic is designed to handle workouts, rain, and everyday adventures.
