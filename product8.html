WZATCO Yuva Infinity Smart Projector (1080P, 4K Support)

The WZATCO Yuva Infinity Smart Projector is designed to bring a cinematic experience to your home. Featuring native 1080P resolution with 4K Ultra HD support, this projector delivers sharp visuals and vibrant colors on large screens up to 300 inches. Its fully automatic setup features such as auto focus and keystone correction make installation effortless. The compact and portable design makes it ideal for home entertainment, movie nights, and casual gaming.
